1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Investment Recommendation: PROCEED WITH CONDITIONS ‚úì

FruitLive represents a compelling investment opportunity in the enterprise agritech SaaS space, addressing a well-defined, high-pain problem in fruit farm operations with a comprehensive, multi-tenant platform solution.

Key Investment Highlights

  • Strong Problem-Solution Fit: Addresses acute pain points around Global GAP compliance (mandatory for export), labor management inefficiencies, and operational complexity in multi-farm operations
  • Quantifiable ROI: Conservative estimates show R600,800 annual value for 500ha operations with 4-6 month payback period
  • Attractive Market: R2.1B SAM in South Africa alone, with clear expansion path to ANZ, Chile, and Southern Europe
  • Defensible Moat: Deep domain expertise, compliance-first architecture, and switching costs create competitive barriers
  • Scalable Architecture: Reusable 'blocks' enable rapid configuration for new customers and automatic system-wide upgrades

Investment Concerns

  • Early Stage Risk: No apparent existing customer base or revenue
  • Regulatory Dependency: Heavy reliance on Global GAP requirements as primary sales driver
  • Implementation Complexity: Comprehensive platform requires significant change management
  • Capital Requirements: High initial development costs for 9-module platform

FruitLive represents a high-potential, moderate-risk opportunity in the underserved agritech compliance software market. The investment case rests on three pillars:

  1. Structural Market Driver: Global GAP v6's digital mandate creates a regulatory forcing function that will drive 85% of South African fruit exporters to digitize within 2-3 years.
